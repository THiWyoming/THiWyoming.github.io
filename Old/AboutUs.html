A Bit About Us

We are a small group, based in SJP II Academy, who are selling premium, handcrafted pallet-style coasters as a way to fundraise for a charity called #TeamSeas. From left to right, our names are Caihlem, Sabastian and Jacob. We are all glad you took the time to visit our website. Caihlem has helped us out throughout the project, creating our prototypes and building many products that allowed us to progress and develop as a team. Sabastian is the financial manager and original creator of our business. He came up with our product and managed everyone so we could meet the time requirements. Jacob is our website developer and marketing manager. He designed the entire website and order form, as well as built many of our pallet-coasters to sell.
